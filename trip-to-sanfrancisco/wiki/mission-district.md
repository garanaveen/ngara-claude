# Mission District

**Summary**: Latino neighborhood with the best food in San Francisco, famous for world-class burritos, murals, and Dolores Park. Where San Franciscans actually eat.

**Sources**: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-13

---

## Overview

The Mission is "where San Franciscans actually eat" and has "the best burritos in the world (not an exaggeration)" according to the source. The neighborhood runs along Valencia and Mission Streets. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Must-Eat Burritos

All three are within blocks of each other, all under $12, all excellent:
- **Taqueria El Farolito** (24th & Mission)
- **La Taqueria** (Mission & 25th)
- **Taqueria Cancun**

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Key Experiences

### Clarion Alley
Walk this alley for politically-charged murals. Runs parallel to 17th St between Valencia & Mission. Worth 20 minutes. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

### Valencia Street
Stroll for bookshops, vintage clothing, and independent restaurants. This is the main commercial corridor. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

### Dolores Park
On a sunny afternoon, this is "peak San Francisco" - half the city is there with picnic blankets, music, and downtown skyline views. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Budget Options

- $4 coffee shops throughout the neighborhood
- $2-3 agua fresca from taqueria windows
- $10-12 for a complete burrito meal
- Natural wine bars available

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Getting There

BART to 16th St Mission or 24th St Mission stations. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Safety Note

Safest during daytime hours; "perfectly fine at night on Valencia St." (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)
