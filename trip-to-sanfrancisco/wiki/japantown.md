# Japantown

**Summary**: Underrated authentic Japanese neighborhood with ramen, karaoke, manga bookstores, and traditional bathhouse. Most tourists skip it, which is exactly why you should go.

**Sources**: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-13

---

## Overview

"Most visitors skip Japantown, which is exactly why you should go." The Japan Center malls offer authentic Japanese experiences without tourist crowds. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## What's There

The Japan Center malls contain:
- Karaoke bars
- Ramen shops
- Manga bookstores (Kinokuniya)
- Japanese snack shops

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Key Destinations

### Marufuku Ramen
- **Style**: Hakata-style tonkotsu
- **Price**: $18
- **Wait time**: Usually 30-45 minutes
- **Verdict**: Worth the queue

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

### Kabuki Springs & Spa
- **Price**: $30
- **Features**: Traditional Japanese communal baths, clothing-optional on designated days

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Practical Details

- **Location**: Japan Center, 1581 Webster St
- **Best time to visit**: Weekends when malls are fully open

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)
