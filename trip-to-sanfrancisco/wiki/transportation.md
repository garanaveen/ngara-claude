# Transportation

**Summary**: San Francisco is very walkable and has excellent public transit. BART connects the airport to downtown for $9.80 vs $45-60 for rideshare. No car needed.

**Sources**: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-13

---

## From SFO Airport to City Center

### BART (Best Option - Always Take This)
- **Cost**: $9.80
- **Time**: 30 minutes to downtown
- **Frequency**: Every 15-20 minutes
- **Hours**: 5am-midnight daily
- **Stations**: Get off at Embarcadero, Montgomery, or Powell Street depending on hotel
- **First step**: Buy a Clipper card at the airport station

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

### Rideshare
- **Cost**: $35-60 depending on traffic
- **Recommendation**: Avoid unless arriving after midnight

### Taxi
- **Cost**: ~$50 + tip
- **Recommendation**: Not worth it

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Getting Around the City

### Muni (Bus + Metro + Streetcar)
- **Cost**: $3/ride or $15/day pass
- **Purchase**: Buy at Walgreens or use MuniMobile app
- **Recommendation**: Get the day pass if taking 5+ rides

### BART
Best for longer cross-city trips (SFO → Mission → Downtown). The urban rail system. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

### Cable Cars
- **Cost**: $9 one-way
- **Lines**: Powell-Hyde, Powell-Mason, California St
- **Recommendation**: "Fun but slow" - worth doing once for the experience

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

### Biking
- **Bay Wheels bikeshare**: $3/30 min
- **Full-day rentals**: $35-45 from shops
- **Best use**: [[golden-gate-bridge]] crossing to Sausalito

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

### Walking
The best way to see neighborhoods, but budget extra time for hills. San Francisco is very walkable in flat areas (Embarcadero, [[north-beach]], [[mission-district]], Castro) but has extreme hills elsewhere. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Clipper Card

Get a Clipper card ($3 for the card) at SFO BART station on arrival. Works for all Muni and BART services with tap-to-pay access. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)
