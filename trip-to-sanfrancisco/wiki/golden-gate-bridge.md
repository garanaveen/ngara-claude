# Golden Gate Bridge

**Summary**: Iconic suspension bridge connecting San Francisco to Marin County, free to walk or bike across with spectacular bay views.

**Sources**: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-13

---

## Overview

Walking the Golden Gate Bridge takes 30-45 minutes one way. The bridge walkway is open daily 5am-9pm. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Best Experience

**Timing**: Go before 9am to beat crowds and get clearer photos. Early morning offers the best visibility. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

**Biking option**: Rent from Bay City Bike near Fisherman's Wharf (~$40/day), ride across to Sausalito (30 min downhill), then return by ferry ($16). This is recommended as better than walking. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Best Viewpoints (All Free)

- **Battery Spencer** (Marin side): The most photographed angle of the bridge
- **Vista Point** (north end): Accessible by car or foot after crossing
- **Crissy Field**: Beach-level perspective

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Practical Details

- **Location**: Golden Gate National Recreation Area
- **Cost**: Free to walk; $10.25 toll if driving southbound
- **Hours**: Walkway open daily 5am-9pm
