# San Francisco with Kids

**Summary**: San Francisco is highly family-friendly with 63+ activities for children, from interactive museums and animal encounters to outdoor play and quirky experiences. Never feels overwhelming, with plenty of outdoor space.

**Sources**: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-13

---

## Overview

San Francisco "packs more distinct neighborhoods, iconic landmarks, and world-class food into 49 square miles" and is "very family-friendly." The city "never felt as overwhelming or busy as some others in America" with "plenty of outdoor space, from parks to beaches." (source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

## Cost-Saving for Families

**City passes**: San Francisco CityPass or Go City Explorer Pass recommended to keep costs down when visiting multiple paid attractions. Both include USS Pampanito, and various museum options. (source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

**Mix of free/paid**: Many top family experiences are free or low-cost, keeping budgets manageable.

## Major Family Attractions

### Animals & Wildlife

**San Francisco Zoo** (Beyond Lake Merced)
- 2,000+ animals including snow leopards, lions, bears, tigers, giraffes
- Many rescued animals
- Included in San Francisco CityPass

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

**Sea Lions at Pier 39** (Free)
- Easy to see, hear, and smell
- Kids can spend longer than expected watching them swim, sunbathe, and scuffle
- See [[free-activities]] for details

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

**Wild Parrots of Telegraph Hill** (Free)
- Cherry-headed conures with vibrant red heads
- Spot near Coit Tower or Sue Bierman Park
- See [[free-activities]]

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

**Bison in [[golden-gate-park]]** (Free)
- Live bison paddock - unexpected urban wildlife

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

### Interactive Museums & Learning

**California Academy of Sciences** ([[golden-gate-park]])
- Planetarium (world's largest all-digital), rainforest, aquarium
- 40,000 animals under one roof
- African Penguin exhibit, 2.5-acre living roof with wildflowers
- "The World's Largest Dinosaurs" exhibition with 60-foot Mamenchisaurus model

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

**Aquarium of the Bay** (Fisherman's Wharf)
- 20,000 marine animals
- Interactive tide pools with seastars (hands-on)
- Behind-the-scenes tour option
- "Discover the Bay" exhibit on local SF marine life

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

**Exploratorium**
- Hands-on science museum (details in source)

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

### Historic & Unique Experiences

**[[alcatraz-island]]**
- "Even young kids will be fascinated"
- "Plenty of space to run around as well as cells to hide in"
- Immersive audio tour helps kids understand history
- "Unexpectedly lovely to wander around on a sunny day"
- See full page for booking details (2+ weeks advance)

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

**USS Pampanito Submarine** (Pier 45)
- WWII submarine museum
- Torpedoes, engine room, mess hall on display
- Included in CityPass/Go City Explorer Pass

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

**Maritime National Historic Park** (Hyde Street Pier)
- Six historic vessels including 19th century square-rigger
- Agents of Discovery game with family missions
- Kid activities available

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

### Entertainment & Play

**Musée Mécanique** (Fisherman's Wharf) - Free entry
- 300+ early-20th century arcade games
- Free to enter, pay per game
- Need change to play

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

**Magowan's Infinite Mirror Maze** (Pier 39)
- Black lights make reflective maze glow
- Free for under-fives

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

**SkyStar Wheel** (Fisherman's Wharf)
- 150 feet high waterfront observation wheel
- Views to North Beach, Russian Hill, Telegraph Hill, Coit Tower, Golden Gate Bridge, Bay Bridge, Sausalito, Alcatraz
- Time for sunset for golden sky views

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

**Carousel** - [[golden-gate-park]]

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

## Outdoor Activities

### Beaches

**Ocean Beach** (western edge)
- Not great for swimming (strong currents)
- Perfect for sandcastles and kite flying

**Baker Beach** (Presidio, southwest corner)
- Mile of sandy shoreline
- Great [[golden-gate-bridge]] and Marin Headlands views
- Note: Northern part is nudist beach
- Swimming not advised for kids

**Crissy Field East Beach**
- Best beach for swimming with kids if weather permits
- Good for surfing too

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

## Special Kid-Friendly Tours

**Vintage Fire Truck Tour** (75 minutes)
- Open-top fire truck from Fisherman's Wharf to Golden Gate Bridge to Fort Baker
- "Plenty of heads turned" - memorable experience
- Kids love the novelty

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

**Scavenger Hunt Walking Tours**
- App-guided Fisherman's Wharf scavenger hunt
- History + landmarks + viewpoints
- Interactive for kids

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

**Big Bus Hop-On Hop-Off**
- 24 stops, family-friendly
- Can upgrade for night tour, Alcatraz, or Golden Gate cruise

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)

## Museums with Family Programs

**De Young Museum** - [[golden-gate-park]]

- Themed around collection/exhibitions

**Asian Art Museum**
- Explorer Packs from family activity table

- Occasional storytelling events

(source: The 63 best things to do in San Francisco with kids.md)
