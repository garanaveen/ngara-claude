# Safety Tips

**Summary**: San Francisco is safe for tourists in most neighborhoods with normal big-city precautions. Main issue is petty theft, especially car break-ins.

**Sources**: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-13

---

## Overall Safety Assessment

"San Francisco is safe for tourists in most neighborhoods, with normal big-city precautions." The city has faced challenges with homelessness and petty crime, but the tourist experience remains excellent. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Main Safety Issue: Car Break-Ins

**Critical warning**: Don't leave anything visible in parked cars. Car break-ins are common citywide and "enforced very loosely." This is the single biggest theft risk. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Petty Theft Hotspots

Pickpocketing happens at:
- Fisherman's Wharf
- Crowded Muni lines

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## The Tenderloin Neighborhood

The Tenderloin (near Union Square) has visible homelessness and drug use. "It's not dangerous in the violent sense but can be uncomfortable." (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Safe Neighborhoods

The [[mission-district]], Castro, [[north-beach]], and all tourist areas are "all fine." (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Practical Safety Tips

- Don't leave a bag unattended
- Use Lyft/Uber at night rather than walking through the Tenderloin
- Keep your phone in your pocket on the cable cars (pickpocket risk)
- Never leave anything visible in a parked car

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Neighborhood-Specific Notes

**Mission District**: "Safest during daytime hours; perfectly fine at night on Valencia St" (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)
