## Overview

This guide provides practical, insider-focused information for visiting San Francisco. It emphasizes what locals actually do rather than pure tourist infrastructure, with specific cost breakdowns, addresses, and booking timelines.

## Key Insights

**Counter-intuitive weather patterns**: September-October is the best time to visit (Indian Summer), while June-July are often cold and foggy. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

**Advance booking critical**: [[alcatraz-island]] tickets sell out 2+ weeks ahead during peak season. Night tours cost $5 extra and require even earlier booking. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

**Tourist trap identification**: Fisherman's Wharf food is explicitly identified as overpriced and mediocre. Real San Francisco food is in [[mission-district]], [[north-beach]], and [[japantown]]. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

**Transportation savings**: BART from SFO costs $9.80 vs $45-60 for rideshare. The guide strongly recommends always taking BART. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Must-Do Experiences (Top 5)

1. Walk or bike the [[golden-gate-bridge]] (free)
2. [[alcatraz-island]] audio tour ($44, book early)
3. Ride a cable car once ($9)
4. Spend an afternoon in [[golden-gate-park]]
5. Eat a Mission-style burrito in [[mission-district]]

These five experiences take about 1.5 days to complete properly. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)
