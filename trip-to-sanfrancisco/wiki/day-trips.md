# Day Trips

**Summary**: Excellent day trip options within 30-90 minutes of San Francisco. Muir Woods, Sausalito, Berkeley/Oakland, Napa Valley, and Point Reyes all accessible.

**Sources**: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-13

---

## Day Trips by Distance

| Destination | Distance | Best Way to Get There | Cost |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sausalito | 30 min | Ferry from Pier 41 | $16 RT |
| Muir Woods | 45 min | Shuttle ($5) or rental car | $20 entry + shuttle |
| Berkeley / Oakland | 30 min | BART ($4.50) | Free (just pay BART) |
| Napa Valley | 1 hour | Rental car or tour ($130+) | $50-200 (tastings vary) |
| Point Reyes | 1.5 hours | Rental car | $30 park entry |

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Sausalito (30 Minutes)

Easy half-day escape by ferry from Pier 41. Can also be reached by biking across [[golden-gate-bridge]] (30 min downhill from bridge), then returning by ferry. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Muir Woods (45 Minutes North)

**Features**: Giant coast redwoods
**Important**: Requires advance parking reservations
**Alternative**: Can be reached by shuttle ($5)

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Berkeley / Oakland (30 Minutes)

Accessible via BART for $4.50 one-way. Free to explore once there - just pay BART fare. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Napa Valley (1 Hour)

The obvious wine country option. Tastings vary $50-200. Need rental car or organized tour ($130+). (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Point Reyes National Seashore (1.5 Hours)

Worth it for remote coastal scenery. Requires rental car. $30 park entry. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)
