# Budget and Costs

**Summary**: San Francisco costs $70-250/person/day depending on travel style. Biggest variable is accommodation. Most top attractions are free or under $20.

**Sources**: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-13

---

## Daily Budget by Travel Style

| Category | Budget ($70/day) | Mid-Range ($200/day) | Splurge ($550/day) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Accommodation | $25-50 (hostel dorm) | $130-180 (budget hotel) | $300-500 (boutique hotel) |
| Food & Drinks | $20-30 (taquerias + groceries) | $60-80 (mix of restaurants) | $130-200 (fine dining) |
| Transport | $7-10 (Muni day pass) | $15-25 (Muni + occasional Lyft) | $40-60 (rideshares) |
| Activities | $10-15 (free parks + 1 paid) | $25-40 (Alcatraz + museum) | $80-100 (tours + tickets) |

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Biggest Cost Variable

Accommodation is the biggest cost variable: $50 hostel dorm vs $250+ hotel room. This determines whether you're on the $70/day budget or $250/day budget more than anything else. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Food Cost Strategy

Food can be cheap if you eat like a local:
- $3 Mission burrito
- $12 pho in the Richmond
- $4 coffee
- $10-15 dim sum on Clement Street

Or expensive if you eat in tourist areas:
- $15+ for mediocre Fisherman's Wharf clam chowder

(source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Major Attraction Costs

- [[alcatraz-island]]: $44.95 adult, $49.95 night tour
- [[golden-gate-park]] museums: $12-25 each
- Cable car: $9/ride
- Museum admission: $10-25

Most top attractions are free or under $20. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)
