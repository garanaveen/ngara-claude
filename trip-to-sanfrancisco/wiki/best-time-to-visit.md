# Best Time to Visit

**Summary**: September-October is best (Indian Summer - warmest weather). June-July are often cold and foggy, surprising many visitors.

**Sources**: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-13

---

## Best Months: September-October

September and October are the best months "by a significant margin" - the city gets its "Indian Summer" with temperatures reaching 65-75°F (18-24°C) while the rest of the country cools down. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Counter-Intuitive Summer Weather

**June and July are often foggy and cool** (60-65°F / 15-18°C). The famous "Karl the Fog" is most active in summer, which surprises many visitors. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Spring (March-May)

Pleasant with mild temperatures and occasional rain. Cherry Blossom Festival in [[japantown]] happens in April. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Winter (November-February)

Mild but rainy. This is the period with lowest prices and fewest crowds. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## What to Bring

Bring or pack a light jacket/fleece - **every season**. San Francisco is rarely warm enough to go without layers. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)

## Special Events

**SF Fleet Week**: October 4-12, 2026. Features Blue Angels air show - free and spectacular. (source: Things to Do in San Francisco_ 2026 Guide to Neighborhoods, Costs & Insider Tips.md)
